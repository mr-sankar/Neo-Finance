About Us

"About Neo Finance: Your Gateway to Transparent Peer-to-Peer Lending, Connecting Investors and Borrowers for Financial Empowerment."

  • Transparent Peer-to-Peer Lending
  • Automated Investment Opportunities
  • Financial Empowerment Through Responsible Lending

Neo Finance is a pioneering peer-to-peer lending platform based in Lithuania, facilitating a direct connection between investors and borrowers. Launched in 2015, Neo Finance focuses on transparent and efficient lending processes, providing individuals with access to personal loans. The platform utilizes a unique scoring model to assess borrower creditworthiness and offers automated investment opportunities for investors.

Services

Neo Finance primarily offers services related to peer-to-peer lending. Here are some key services provided by Neo Finance:

Peer-to-Peer Lending

Facilitating direct lending and borrowing relationships between individuals, Neo Finance allows users to access personal loans or invest in loans offered by others.

Automated Investment

Investors can use the platform's automated investment feature, which streamlines the investment process by allocating funds based on predefined criteria, reducing manual intervention.

Loan Scoring Model

Neo Finance employs a unique scoring model to assess the creditworthiness of borrowers, providing investors with information to make informed decisions on loan investments.

Transparent Loan Information

The platform prioritizes transparency, offering detailed information about loan terms, interest rates, and borrower profiles, enabling users to make well-informed financial decisions.
